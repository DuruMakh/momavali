Government finance at roughly $10k real GDP per capita through 1990

Spending and Revenue show each country only if its matched year was 1990 or earlier: the year where Maddison real GDP per capita was closest to 10,000 international dollars in 2011 prices, within the 9,000-11,000 band. REVENUE OECD uses 1965 OECD tax revenue (% of GDP) against 1965 Maddison GDP per capita, keeping only countries with both values. Kuwait is excluded from the threshold views.
